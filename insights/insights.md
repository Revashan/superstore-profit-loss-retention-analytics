# Business Insights

## Key Findings

Technology category generates the highest profitability.

Furniture category shows negative margins due to heavy discounting.

Tables and bookcases are the most loss-making products.

West region contributes the highest revenue.

Customer retention is strong among corporate segment buyers.

## Business Recommendations

Reduce excessive discounting in furniture category.

Optimize pricing strategy for tables and bookcases.

Focus marketing campaigns in high-performing regions.

Implement loyalty programs to increase customer retention.
